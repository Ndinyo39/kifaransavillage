Kifaransa village is an organisation formed with the aim of promoting the French language by bringing together Francophiles and giving them the opportunity to meet.

About Us

The KIFARANSA VILLAGE Was founded in 2020 by Mr. Sammy SIMIYU and co- founders devoted to promote French language, encourage reciprocal communication between all levels of learning it and reuniting the french community and those who have intrest and passion to learn with benefits that Come with learning french.
The Kifaransa Village was incorporated in 2022 and, after established opérations in Nairobi in patnership with Alliance Française , it extended its opérations to Coastal and Western/lake region adopting regional strategy under the regional exécutive officials in the other regions.

Aims

Objectives

The Kifaransa Village is an inclusive association, which seeks to build, value, honor and diversity in French language. Associations that are diverse in age, race, ethnicity, faith, gender identity, , and perspectives are better associations. To this end, we seek to create a community where educators, from any background, can promote French language around the world.

KIFARANSA VILLAGE offers the invaluable opportunity to explore the language in real live situations by engaging with French speakers and exploring this cauthentic situations such as presentations, expositions, talents and music. It is a wonderful vehicle for bringing the subject of French to life as it reinforces what is learnt in the classroom through meaningful, unforgettable experiences with the aim of promoting the French language by bringing together Francophiles and giving them the opportunity.

  • To meet and Gather the French family;Family members to interact as it members from each other,networking and sharing French expériences.
  • To engage and share talents (music, poetry, storytelling, writing, photography, social media, organizing events, etc.)
  • To highlight talents mainly in French in the broad sense.
  • To engage the community, prove to them that the foreign language is worth their time, money and resources because it contributes to our daily lives and also matters to our future.
  • To facilitate language learning and teaching in différent ways and Mastery as it encourages reciprocal communication between all levels of French in terms of learning and teaching.
